Service

Three Phases to Success

Phase 1: Preparation

We identify real demand, competitive gaps, and revenue potential — before you invest in execution.

Market Research

  • Assess market size, demand, and growth potential in Japan.
  • Analyze the competitive landscape and category dynamics.
  • Evaluate pricing levels and margin structure to understand profitability.

Product-Market Fit Check

  • Assess how the brand's value, features, and pricing resonate in Japan.
  • Determine the level of localization required to compete effectively.
  • Clarify points of differentiation versus competitors.

GTM Strategy Design

  • Set localization direction and depth required for Japan
  • Design funnel-based marketing and sales actions by channel.
  • Set execution roadmaps with clear KPIs, budgets, timelines, and resource plans.

Phase 2: Execution

We adapt your product to what works in Japan — and execute rapidly in the real market.

Localization

  • Messaging & Communication — Tone, trust, information density, and cultural nuance
  • UX & Interface Adaptation
  • Creative & Visual Direction
  • Brand Guardrails — Clear boundaries to preserve global brand intent

Execution

Full-funnel digital execution to test, learn, and iterate in-market

  • Paid ads (Meta, Google, LINE)
  • Website, LP creation, SEO
  • Social media operation
  • Influencer & PR activation
  • CRM workflow (E-mail, LINE, MA)

Phase 3: Scale

Build sustainable growth and expand market share through scalable strategy.

Sales Enablement

  • Activate local sales execution by collaborating with trusted Japanese sales agencies for scalable reach.
  • Design sales workflows from lead sourcing through closing and onboarding.
  • Build customer support foundations, including Japanese-language support teams, onboarding flows, and close alignment with sales.

Optimization

  • Optimize performance across the full funnel by improving KPIs across marketing, sales, CRM, and customer success.
  • Drive distribution growth through strategic partnerships and alliances.
  • Maximize long-term value by driving retention and LTV through CRM and strategic initiatives.
